1 Qualification Levels




Key Trends

The proportion of working-age residents with level 4+ qualifications in LCR is 29.1%. This is -14.2% lower than the national average.

The proportion of those with no qualifications in LCR is 21.1%. This is 16.6% higher than the national average.



2 Apprenticeships
